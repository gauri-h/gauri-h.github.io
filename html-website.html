The Inheritance Games

Book Cover

The Inheritance Games is my absolute favorite book series of all time! If you haven't read it yet, you're missing out on an amazing adventure.

Why I Love This Series:

This thrilling series by Jennifer Lynn Barnes follows Avery Grambs, who unexpectedly inherits billions from a stranger, Tobias Hawthorne. The catch? She must live in his mansion with his family, including his four grandsons who are determined to solve the puzzle of why she got the fortune instead of them.

My Review:

The books are full of puzzles, riddles, and mysteries that keep you guessing until the very end. I love trying to solve the clues alongside the characters! The character development is amazing, and the plot twists will leave you shocked.

Books in the Series:

  • The Inheritance Games
  • The Hawthorne Legacy
  • The Final Gambit
